School Projects

RochamBEAU

3D printed hand actuated using three strings and one servo motor that plays Rock Paper Scissors with a user wearing a glove with flex sensors sewn into the index and ring finger. Project for CMSC 20380 (Actuated User Interfaces and Technology) at UChicago, 2025. Video on Youtube

Actuated Exoplanet Orrery

Actuated and interactive orrery made using Toio robots to physicalize exoplanetary systems. Group Project with Jun Asano, Dillon Bass, and Holly Wei, for CMSC 20380 (Actuated User Interfaces and Technology) at UChicago, 2025. Video on Youtube

Anti-Soap Dispenser

Device that prevents users from getting more soap within 20 seconds of the previous time they got some, and uses a light to inform the user that they've washed their hands within the last 10 minutes. Designed with those with Obsessive Compulsive Disorder in mind. Project for CMSC 20380 (Actuated User Interfaces and Technology) at UChicago, 2025. Video on Youtube

Letterboxd Notification Device

Device shaped like a mailbox and a clapperboard whose clapper stick raises when someone you follow logs a movie on letterboxd. Assignment for CMSC 20380 (Actuated User Interfaces and Technology) at UChicago, 2025. Video on Youtube

Shadows of Greece's Past

3D model in Blender where a wall is built so that with each layer, a new shadow is cast that builds off of the previous one. Assignment for SOSC 19009 (Greek Antiquity and its Legacy-III) at UChicago, 2024. Link to Google Slides

Sprout Journal

Prototype for an app designed to improve college students' mental health through journaling. Group project with Thuc Dzu, Quinn Thompson, Mai Vu, and Emily Zen for CMSC 20370 (Inclusive Technology: Designing for Underserved and Marginalized Populations), at UChicago, 2024. Video on Youtube
